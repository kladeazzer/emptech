Prevention

Here, there are some tips and warning that is included to help the people prevent and be resilient when it comes to hazardous happenings:

- Clear up the drainage system by eliminating the possible blockage.

- Proper segregation for avoiding littering.

- Improve urban drainage, including pumping station.

- Improve flood forecasting, early warning systems, community-based flood risk management.
